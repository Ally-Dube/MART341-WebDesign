Baked Brussel Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds Brussel Sprouts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Splash of lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese

How to make Baked Brussel Sprouts

  1. Preheat oven to 425oF.
  2. Trim the bottom of the sprouts and remove loose outer leaves. Cut each Brussel sprout in half.
  3. Place the sprouts on a rimmed and linned baking sheet. Add the olive oil and evenly coat the sprouts.
  4. Season the sprouts with the salt, pepper, and garlic.
  5. Place all sprouts cut side down.
  6. Place the butter sporadically on the pan.
  7. Place the pan in the oven on the top rack. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Let the sprouts cool and place them in a medium size bowl.
  9. Pour in the balsamic vinegar, lemon, and cheese. Mix and serve!
