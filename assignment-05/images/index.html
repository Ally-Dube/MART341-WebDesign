The Southern Book Clubs Guide To Slaying Vampires

Grady Hendrix

The Southern Book Clubs Guide To Slaying Vampires is a fictional mystery book about women overcomming their roles in sociecty to save their children and the community when a new threat arises.

The women have to face challenges of sociecty views and a monster simotaneously, all while upholding the perfect housewife image.
