Recipe for Chicken Parmesan

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425oF.
  2. Prepare homade red sauce or have canned sauce ready.
  3. Prepare two chicken breasts by pounding them to an even thickeness (about 1/2inch thick).
  4. Salt and pepper both sides of the chicken.
  5. Prepare an egg wash with two eggs in a bowl.
  6. In a seperate bowl, mix two cups of panko bread crumbs with shredded parmesan cheese.
  7. In another bowl, pour a small amount of flour.
  8. Begin by dipping a chicken breast in flour, then in egg, lastly in the bread crumb mixture.
  9. Repeat with other chicken.
  10. Let the chicken rest for a minute and begin boiling water in a pot - this is for spagetti to go with the chicken parmesan.
  11. Prepare a nonstick oven pan by coating generously with olive oil.
  12. Place the chicken on the nonstick pan and put in the oven when water is boiling.
  13. Set a timer for 7min - this is for the chicken - when the 7min is up, pull the chicken out and flip it. Put it back in for 5min.
  14. Add spagetti to a pot and boil till aldente.
  15. When chicken is done baking pull it out of the oven.
  16. Set the oven to broil.
  17. Put a spoonful of sauce and then mozerella on chicken.
  18. Put the chicken back in the oven for 3-5min or until cheese is golden brown.
  19. When spagetti is done, drain it and put the pasta back into pot with butter.
  20. When the chicken and pasta are both done, plate both together and add sauce per preferance, add basil and shredded parmesan cheese for an extra touch.


Ingredients:

  • Two chicken breasts
  • Two eggs
  • Panko bread crumbs (italian style)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Slices of fresh mozerella
  • Red sauce (homeade or canned)
  • Fresh basil
  • Spagetti
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
