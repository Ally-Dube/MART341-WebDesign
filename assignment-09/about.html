About




Why did I choose this project?

Cooking is a big part of my life and growing up my favorite home-cooked meal was chicken parmesan. My mother used to cook this for me on any special occasion and once I moved out, I learned my own version of the recipe. I think its a classic dish that many love and enjoy so I wanted to share my recipe!




Tips and tricks to make this recipe better!

Make sure to pound your chicken out to a consistent size - this will help it cook evenly. Homade red sauce will definitely wow your guests but jar sauce is a quick subsitute. Dont over-cook your chicken. The more mozerella, the better! Broiling at the end will make your melted cheese picture perfect. Fresh basil will make your dish even better.
