Dazed and Confused













This website represents a place for my small collection called "Dazed and Confused". Its a collection I put together during my sophmore year of college. Each piece is acrylic on canvas ranging in sizes. This collection is an investigation on confusion and understanding, I wanted to explore what a vibrant color palette can do to a potentially unsettling scene. I found that color really can change the mood of a piece and make it something entirely different.
-Allyson Dube
