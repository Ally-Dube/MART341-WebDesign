Alone and Unknown




My Orignal Painting



Acrylic on canvas by Ally Dube






This is the first piece in the collection called "Alone and Unknown". I think, looking at this piece, compared to the others you can tell its the first one in the series as its not as clean. I do enjoy the color palette for this piece and the edges of the canvas are painted a deep vibrant red which translates well when hanging. I wanted this piece to contain confusion with all the entry ways and the "empty" ladder. Its unknown where the entry ways go and the ladder is, in theory, unusable. How does this piece make you feel?
