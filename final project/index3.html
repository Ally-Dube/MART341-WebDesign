Empty and Anxious




My Orignal Painting



Acrylic on canvas by Ally Dube






This is the second piece in the collection called "Empty and Anxious". Its the smallest piece in the series and the edges are painted a beige/cream color that compliment the piece really well. It is the simplest of the pieces and thats where I wanted the empty feeling to play in. Like all the pieces, I wanted to include an object that is a little bit off so we get the chair with a missing leg. The color palette is more muted and cool toned compared to the others which gives it a unique touch. How does this piece make you feel?
